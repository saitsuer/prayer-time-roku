# Prayer Time

**A beautiful Islamic prayer times screensaver for Roku.**

Prayer Time turns your Roku TV into an elegant prayer companion. Display the
five daily prayer times for your location, with stunning panoramic
backgrounds, the call to adhan at each prayer, and rotating verses, hadith,
and wisdom from the Risale-i Nur.

[![Available on Roku Channel Store](https://img.shields.io/badge/Roku-Channel%20Store-purple)](https://channelstore.roku.com)

---

## Features

- **Accurate prayer times** — Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, plus Imsak and sunrise
- **Automatic location** — detects your city via IP, or pick manually from a worldwide list
- **Full adhan audio** at every prayer time, with optional post-adhan supplication
- **Daily content** — rotating verses from the Qur'an, authentic hadith, and Risale-i Nur
- **Dual calendar** — Gregorian and Hijri dates side by side
- **Five calculation methods** — Diyanet, ISNA, MWL, Umm al-Qura, Egyptian
- **Silent mode** for quiet hours
- **Bilingual** — English and Turkish interface

## How to install

1. Open the [Roku Channel Store](https://channelstore.roku.com) on your Roku device
2. Search for **Prayer Time**
3. Add the channel

Or use the direct install code:

> **Access code:** `PRAYERTIME`

Visit [my.roku.com/account/add/PRAYERTIME](https://my.roku.com/account/add/PRAYERTIME)

## How to use

1. Open the app — you'll see the settings panel
2. Configure your preferences:
   - Pick your city
   - Choose a calculation method
   - Turn silent mode on or off
   - Toggle the post-adhan dua
3. Set as your screensaver:
   **Settings → Theme → Screensaver → Prayer Time**

The screensaver activates automatically when your Roku is idle.

## Calculation methods

| Method | Best for |
|---|---|
| Diyanet | Turkey |
| ISNA | North America |
| MWL (Muslim World League) | Europe, general use |
| Umm al-Qura | Saudi Arabia |
| Egyptian | Egypt, parts of Africa |

If you're not sure which to pick, **MWL** is a reasonable default for most regions.

## Privacy

Prayer Time **does not collect any personal information**. Your preferences
are stored only on your Roku device. The app contacts two external services
solely to provide its functionality:

- [ip-api.com](https://ip-api.com) — to detect your city
- [aladhan.com](https://aladhan.com) — for prayer time calculations

See the full [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.html) and [Terms of Use](TERMS.html).
