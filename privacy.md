# Privacy Policy — Prayer Time

Last updated: May 19, 2026

## What we collect

Prayer Time is a Roku screensaver application. **We do not collect, store, 
or transmit any personally identifiable information** about you or your 
device.

## How the app works

To show accurate prayer times for your location, the app makes the following 
network requests:

1. **Geolocation**: A request to ip-api.com to determine your approximate 
   city based on your IP address. Only the city name and coordinates are 
   used; no IP is logged by us.
2. **Prayer times**: A request to api.aladhan.com to fetch daily prayer 
   times for your selected location.

All requests are made over HTTPS. No data is sent to any server operated by 
us, because we operate no server.

## Local storage

Your preferences (language, calculation method, selected city, silent mode, 
post-adhan dua) are stored locally on your Roku device using Roku's built-in 
registry. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-party services

- **ip-api.com** — see their privacy policy at https://ip-api.com/docs/legal
- **aladhan.com** — see their privacy policy at https://aladhan.com/privacy-policy

## Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data.
